Terms of Use

Last updated: April 25, 2026

Service Summary

Avelune is a personal planning app that helps users manually build time blocks, run focus sessions, review simple planning insights, and optionally view a read-only overlay of device calendar events while planning.

Subscription Offering

Avelune Pro Yearly is an auto-renewable subscription. The subscription title, duration, and current price are shown inside the app’s purchase flow before checkout. If a free trial is offered, it is displayed in the app and configured through Apple App Store Connect.

Renewal And Cancellation

The subscription renews automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the end of the current billing period. Users can manage or cancel subscriptions in Apple ID account settings after purchase.

Restore Purchases

Users who previously purchased Avelune Pro can restore access by using the Restore Purchases action available in the paywall and Settings.

Feature Access

Some features such as unlimited blocks, calendar overlay, Home Screen widgets, and extended focus history or insights are part of Avelune Pro. Core planning features remain available without a subscription.

Availability

Avelune is provided as-is and may evolve over time. Feature availability can change as the app is updated.
